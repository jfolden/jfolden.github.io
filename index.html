About

I lead the Algorithm Group at Quidient where we work on generalized scene reconstruction. Previously, I received my PhD from the FOCUS Lab at the University of Florida working with Sanjeev Koppal on computer vision and computational photography. During my PhD I had the chance to intern at Snap Research, Meta Reality Labs, and NASA Intelligent Robotics Group. At Snap Research, I joined Jian Wang and published a CVPR 2023 paper on a holographic depth camera for Snap Spectacles AR glasses. At Meta Reality Labs, I joined Shuochen Su and developed an efficient ML stereo depth algorithm for Meta AR/VR headsets (patent pending). At NASA, I joined Uland Wong and developed an OptiX rendering engine to generate synthetic data for their 3D reconstruction imaging systems. I am broadly interested in visual computing, from silicon to pixels.
